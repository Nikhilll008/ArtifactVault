About the Museum

A Repository of Maharashtra's Cultural Memory

Established under the patronage of the Directorate of Archaeology & Museums, the Maharashtra Heritage Museum has spent over two decades documenting the state's Maratha-era forts, weaponry, coinage and textile traditions. ArtifactVault extends this mission into a searchable digital archive, accessible to students, researchers and the general public.

Beyond display, the museum actively manages a loan programme that circulates artifacts to partner institutions, schools and cultural bodies across the state for exhibitions and research.
